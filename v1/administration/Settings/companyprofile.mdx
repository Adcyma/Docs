# Company Profile

import Image from '@theme/IdealImage';

The Company Profile page shows your organization's registered information in Adcyma, including identification details and contact info used for communication and support.

## What You Can View

As an administrator, you can view these identifiers:

- Company Name
- Adcyma Company ID

## What You Can Manage

You can edit the following contact and location fields:

- Street
- Zip Code
- City
- State
- Country
- First Contact Person
- First Contact Email
- First Contact Phone

## Purpose and Usage

The Adcyma support team uses your company profile to identify your organization and reach the right contacts. Keep this information up to date so support requests and communications go to the right place.
