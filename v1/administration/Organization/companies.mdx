# Companies

This page lets you manage the list of companies available when provisioning users in Entra ID. Companies are initially pulled from your Entra ID configuration during setup.

## Managing Your Company List

From this page, you can:
- View all companies in your list
- Add new companies
- Remove companies you no longer need

After making changes, click **Save Changes** to commit your updates.

## Initial Synchronization

Your company data is imported from Entra ID during setup. Keep in mind:
- This is a one-time import, not an ongoing sync
- After the initial import, you manage the list manually
- Future changes in Entra ID will not automatically update this list
