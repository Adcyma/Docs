# Companies

This page lets you manage the companies in your organization's directory. Companies are initially pulled from your Entra ID configuration.

## Managing Your Company List

From this page, you can:
- View all companies in your organization
- Add new companies
- Remove companies you no longer need
- Review company details

## Saving Changes

**Important:** After adding or removing companies, click **Save Changes** to commit your updates. Changes are not automatically saved and will be lost if you navigate away without saving.

## Initial Synchronization

Your company data is imported from Entra ID during setup. Keep in mind:
- This is a one-time import, not an ongoing sync
- After the initial import, you manage the list manually
- Future changes in Entra ID will not automatically update this list
